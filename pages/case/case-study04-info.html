About this project

GigHub app
Intro

This project exam focuses on developing GigHub, a user-friendly, intuitive, and innovative app tailored to support digital nomads in finding work efficiently and help maintain remote productivity, maintain a healthy balance between professional life and personal life but also expand their connection ... Over a ten-week design process, I will conduct in-depth research to explore the unique needs, preferences, and challenges faced by digital nomads that form the foundation for generating ideas, building a prototype, and testing it to gather valuable insight into the app users' needs and preferences. The goal is also to create an app that helps digital nomads combat the challenges of freelancing and remote work to be able to thrive professionally and personally regardless of where they are in the world.

Explore, search, filter and find gigs is just few clicks away with GigHub!
Gigs

The app provides a comprehensive list of gigs from different technology fields that align with the digital nomad skills and preferences ... Users have the flexibility to search and filter gig listing based on location, industry field, project duration, job type, experience level, salary, skill and company type. They can save gigs to their favorite list by using their Iphone or Iwatch and apply for work by email. Making it easy, efficient for digital nomads to find relevant work opportunities reducing the stress of job searching and applying for work. Digital nomads can easily stay financially stable and focus on the important part which is building their careers, connection and experiencing the world. .

Simplify, organize, track, and deliver on time every time with GigHub app!
Calendar

The app provides project management tools that help digital nomad structure and organize their work ... Digital nomads can track their work progress, keep deadlines and stay on top of tasks. This feature help streamline their workflow, reduce stress and make sure that they don't miss project deadlines and help digital nomad stay productive and deliver high quality work on time regardless of where they are in the world

Stay synchronized and stay productive with GigHub app!
Time Zone

Gig Hub provides a time zone tool that will help digital nomad work across multiple time zones ... Digital nomad can track multiple time zones in real time making it easier to collaborate with clients and teams across the world but also never miss a meeting or deadline. This feature helps digital nomads plan their work efficiently, stay productive and avoid confusion with double booked meetings.

Connect, Work, Thrive, Stay Linked and Stay Productive with GigHub app
Connectivity

The app provides Internet connectivity and co working space map feature that helps digital nomad find the best place to work that offers reliable internet worldwide ... Digital nomad can easily locate nearby co-working spaces, cafes and public spaces with reliable internet connection. The map provides user rating, detailed information about each location around the world. This feature is important for digital nomads to be able to work and collaborate with other team members digitally efficiently. Digital nomads can now find the best location for work effectively and transition between different environments without losing productivity.

Decompress, recharge, perform, achieve balance and Achieve Success, thrive professionally and personally with GigHub app
Mental Health

Working remotely can lead to stress due to tight project deadlines, managing multiple projects, loneliness and staying financially secure ... With GigHub digital nomad can perform a variety of different breathing exercises and meditation sessions making it easier for users to decompress and stay focused. Users can access daily mood logs, gratitude journals and sleep logs. This feature helps digital nomads to prioritize their well being. Cause true success is not just about finding gigs or completing gigs but it's about achieving a healthy lifestyle that will have a positive impact on both personal and professional growth.

Summary for X-plore app semester project
Reflection

This project allowed me to enhance my creative thinking and user-centric design skills ... One of the challenges I encountered while designing the X-plore app was how to create an engaging and intuitive environmental learning app, with a specific focus on the declining bee and bat population, targeted both environmental enthusiasts and users unfamiliar with this subject. My lack of personal experience and knowledge in this field made it difficult to address the unique needs, emotions, and challenges of potential users of this app. This area was relatively new to me. Despite the challenges, this project was both demanding and enjoyable. It enabled me to immerse myself in the users' context, their frustrations, and motivations through research, which provided a solid foundation for the design process. Through a combination of literature reviews and interviews, I gathered essential insights to create a user persona. Competitor and SWOT analyses also contributed to a comprehensive understanding of this project. This research phase facilitated the generation of a problem statement that guided subsequent design steps, including ideation and prototyping. This process was personally fulfilling as it led to a solution that genuinely met users' needs. Reflecting on the project with more time, I would focus on implementing additional gamification elements and rewards to motivate users to contribute to the environment, engage in more challanges, quizzes, create and participate in enviromental events. But also develop feature that allows users to calculate the environmental impact of different choices, such as their transportation mode or diet. This can help them make more informed decisions. These feature will enhance the overall user experience of the app.
