Goals

1. Understand the core target audience of this app, the digital nomad demographics. Their specific needs, motivation pain points, frustration, goals, and preferences while choosing a remote job, how do these challenges impact a person's overall well being

2. What is the main motivation factor that makes a person decide to choose a digital nomad lifestyle

3. How does changing location affect a person's work and personal life related to isolation, stress and loneliness

4. Understand what kind of tools, platform and device are being used when searching for work. How productive are these tools in assisting finding potential job

5. Identify key features and customization option that users are looking for in an work search app

6. determine which competitors currently offer a product that solves this issue, their feature, and how satisfied are the user with these apps

Project timeline

A project timeline is an important tool that helps to make sure that each step of the design thinking process is covered when developing the GigHub app ... The project timeline helps the project progress smoothly without missing any deadlines. It helps me plan, break the project into manageable tasks, stay on track, set clear goals and deadlines. I am able to prioritize how much time to use on each task, prioritize time on task. This way, I can focus on critical tasks and meet project goals and delivery dates productively without being afraid of missing any important part. Instead of keeping track manually this timeline helps me to work effectively and structuring the time correctly when working on a large project like this .
