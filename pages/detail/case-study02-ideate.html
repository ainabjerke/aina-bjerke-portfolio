NUF test

The NUF Test is a tool for evaluating design ideas, particularly those generated through brainstorming and mind mapping. Assessing novelty keeps the design process dynamic, while prioritizing usefulness avoids investing in irrelevant features. Evaluating feasibility early avoids pursuing unviable ideas, considering technology, resources, and time constraints ... It helps strike a balance between innovation and practicality in UX design. It ensures that ideas are not just creative but also user-centric, considering their usefulness and feasibility. This helps avoid pursuing unviable concepts and focuses on creating user-centric solutions. In discussions, personas and empathy maps were used to align solutions with user preferences and understand user experiences, emphasizing the emotional aspects of interactions in selecting top concepts and ideas.
