Information

According to a small survey conducted by Baby Centre in the UX 40% of mothers admitted to feeling self-conscious in front of their partners. Around one in seven women slips into postpartum depression and experience physical changes. A lot of experts, therefore, recommended exercising because postnatal exercise helps mothers to get their confidence and firm up their body

Goals

1. To identify who is the core target audience of this app

2. To understand the specific needs, pain points, frustration, goals, context, and preferences of this core target user

3. To determine which competitors currently offer a product that solves this issue, their feature, and how satisfied are the user with these apps

4. To identify the key features that users are looking for in an exercise app

5. To identify the customization option that the user would like to see in an online exercise app
